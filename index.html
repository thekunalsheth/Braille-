What is braille?

Created by: Kunal, Vignesh, Asif, and Abi

We just wanted to create an application dedicated to help the blind. Why not have a tool that aids them with navigation? Our application allows users to take pictures of their surrounding and intelligently detect and recognize the objects infront of them. Our application uses available AI tools to convert recognized objects from a text name into verbal commands.

"Our technology helps people see with their ears"
